I am currently working on the following topics:

• Cohomological Donaldson-Thomas theory for real 3 manifolds: Currently the goal is to prove the cohomological integrality conjecture for the of stack of local systems on the 3 Torus.
• Formulating and proving cohomological integrality for real 3 manifolds for general reductive groups. This also allows to explore the connections to existing 3 manifold invariants such as Skein modules

Other projects

• Brill--Noether theory for Kuznetsov components and refined categorical Torelli theorems for index one Fano threefolds (with Zhiyu Liu and Shizhuo Zhang), arXiv, pdf, submitted.
